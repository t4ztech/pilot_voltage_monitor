
SCOPE OF WORK — VOLTAGE OBSERVABILITY PILOT

IN SCOPE
- Read-only monitoring of voltage behavior
- Analysis of historical and simulated measurement data
- Detection of abnormal patterns and trends
- Software-based alerts and summary reports
- Interpretation of results and recommendations

OUT OF SCOPE
- Any form of control or actuation
- Modifications to PLC logic or configuration
- Interaction with safety or protection systems
- Responsibility for physical damage or equipment failure
- Real-time operational decision-making

AUTHORITIES
- Safety systems retain the highest authority
- PLC logic remains deterministic and unchanged
- Monitoring software has advisory status only
DESIGN PRINCIPLE
Observation precedes intervention.
Safety precedes automation.
